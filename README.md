I build bioinformatics tools and web platforms that make genomic surveillance accessible — for public health labs in LMICs as much as for major reference centres. My work spans **population genomics**, **AMR surveillance**, **genome QC**, and **open-source tooling**.

I contribute to [Pathogenwatch](https://pathogen.watch), [EnteroBase](https://enterobase.warwick.ac.uk), and [AllTheBacteria](https://allthebacteria.org), and I'm part of the **WHO Collaborating Centre for Genomic Epidemiology** at [CGPS Oxford](https://www.cgps.group). I also co-host the **[MicroBinfie Podcast](https://www.happykhan.com/microbinfie)** — exploring the fast-evolving world of microbial bioinformatics.
